Finance Sales & Profitability (SQL • Python • Power BI)

Star schema (Sales, Products, Customers, Calendar). KPIs: Revenue, Gross Margin %, Average Selling Price (ASP), Year-over-Year growth, and Last-3-Months performance. Python EDA for outliers & seasonality. Power BI with drill-through from Country → Region → Customer → Product.

  • SQL: Common Table Expressions (CTEs) for time-intelligence and gross margin calculations.
  • Python: Pandas-based EDA, monthly trend checks, and sanity checks on revenue & cost distributions.
  • BI: Power BI report with bookmarks, tooltip pages, and a robust Date table powered by DAX.

Sales & profitability dashboard showing revenue, cost, profit margin, regional trends, and top-performing products.

Business Problem

The regional finance team needed a unified, trusted view of revenue, gross margin %, and product mix to replace multiple Excel files and manual slide decks. The goal was to quickly answer: “Which regions and products drive profitability over time, and where are we losing margin?”

Data

  • Sales fact table: ~250k rows over 3 years, one row per invoice line.
  • Dimensions: Products, Customers, Calendar (role-playing dates for Order Date and Ship Date).
  • Source: CSV exports simulating ERP extracts, cleaned and validated with Python & SQL before loading into BI.

Solution

  • Modeled a star schema with a reusable Date dimension and conformed dimensions for Products and Customers.
  • Wrote SQL to compute base metrics (net sales, discounts, cost of goods) and staged them into reporting tables.
  • Used Python (Pandas) to validate aggregate totals, check monthly seasonality, and detect outlier transactions before reporting.
  • Built a Power BI report with drill-through from Region → Customer → Product, and DAX time-intelligence for MTD/QTD/YTD and YoY comparisons.

Key Insights

  • Identified that ~15% of SKUs contributed close to 70% of revenue (classic Pareto pattern), guiding focus for promotions and stock planning.
  • Found two regions with strong revenue but weak gross margins due to heavy discounting and freight costs.
  • Created a reusable Date table and core KPI measures that can be plugged into future finance and sales reports.

Deliverables

  • SQL scripts: Staging logic and KPI calculation queries for revenue, margin, and ASP.
  • Python notebook: EDA notebook for validation, seasonal trend checks, and outlier detection.
  • Power BI report (PBIX) or screenshots: Final interactive dashboard with drill-through and tooltips.

If This Were in Production

  • Schedule automated refreshes (daily/weekly) via an ETL/ELT pipeline with data quality checks.
  • Add row-level security (RLS) so regional managers only see their markets.
  • Instrument usage metrics to see which pages and filters are most used by stakeholders.
